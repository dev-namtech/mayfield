Design Development & Prototyping

Mayfield have come a long way from our humble beginnings in the Glenferrie Markets of Melbourne. Originally making lampshades for local retailers, the Mayfield name now encompasses a wide range of high-quality homewares and lighting solutions, and we’re constantly growing. Business trades and secrets being passed down through generations, Mayfield is now illuminating the furniture and lighting industry.

Production

Mayfield’s own range of table and floor lamps can be specified as standard, developed to suit your requirements, or we can manufacture to your specification.

Drawing on our industrial design and manufacturing expertise, Mayfield will work with your design team to provide customised, and fit for purpose lighting solutions to your next project. Our lighting expertise includes table lamps, floor lamps, lamp shades, pendant lights, wall lights and decorative accessories.

Handcrafted

Handmade by skilled crafts people at our Melbourne factory, our lampshades and pendant fittings are available in a range of forms and sizes. Fabrics can be selected from our exclusive range, or specified from any of the leading wall covering and fabric houses.

Our Clients

Mayfield design and produce contemporary light fittings for hotels, aged care facilities, serviced apartments, conference centres, restaurants and cafes. We pride ourselves on our short leadtimes, competitive pricing, and honest and open communication with all project stakeholders.
