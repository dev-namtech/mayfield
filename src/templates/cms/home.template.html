Our Story

From humble beginnings in 1945, we've become
a renowned supplier of the Australian furniture and lighting industry.

The Mayfield Lamps story began amongst the stalls of Melbourne’s Glenferrie Markets.

From this humble start over 70 years ago, Mayfield Lamps has become one of Australia’s most well-respected suppliers in furniture and lighting.

Today, Mayfield offers more than just lamps – we now design and produce fashionable light fittings, furniture and accessories for both the retail and commercial markets across Australia.
